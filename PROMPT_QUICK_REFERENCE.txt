================================================================================
CLASSIFICATION KEYWORDS
================================================================================

ROOT CAUSE:
  Keywords: why, reason, cause, risky, risk, problem, issue
  Exclude: "not" (that's why-not)

COMPARISON:
  Keywords: compare, vs, versus, difference, similar

WHY-NOT (STABILITY):
  Keywords: why + not (both required)
  Examples: "why not", "why is not", "why isn't"

TRACEABILITY:
  Keywords: show, top, contributing, records, impacted, affected

SUMMARY:
  Default for unclassified queries
  Keywords: status, overview, summary, health
